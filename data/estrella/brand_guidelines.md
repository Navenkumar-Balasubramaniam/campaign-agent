# Estrella Damm — Brand Guidelines (Academic Reference)

> Academic reference document compiled from public Estrella Damm / Damm Corporate
> communications for this university project. Not an official brand-approved document.
> See `PROVENANCE.md` for sources.

## Brand essence
Estrella Damm is a Barcelona-born Mediterranean lager (founded 1876). The brand
positions itself as the beer of the **Mediterranean way of life** — sunshine,
the sea, friends, food, music, and slowing down to enjoy "the little things".

## Positioning statement
The beer that celebrates the Mediterranean lifestyle: social, sunny, authentic,
and increasingly committed to protecting the Mediterranean environment.

## Tone of voice
- Warm, human, cinematic, and emotional
- Social and inclusive — moments shared with friends
- Summery and relaxed; never aggressive or hard-sell
- Authentic and rooted in real places (Catalonia, the Balearics, the coast)
- Responsible: drinking is social, never excessive

## Signature themes ("Mediterráneamente")
- Summer, sunlight, the sea and the coast
- Friendship, reunion, nostalgia, "the little things"
- Music (each summer film carries a signature track)
- Mediterranean food and gastronomy
- Environmental commitment (protecting the sea, ending plastic pack rings)

## Visual identity
- Warm natural daylight, golden-hour tones
- Coastal and outdoor settings: beaches, terraces, beer gardens, small coastal towns
- Cinematic, film-like compositions (the summer ad is treated as a short film)
- Clear product presence (the iconic Estrella Damm bottle / star logo) without it dominating the emotion
- Red and gold brand colours; the five-pointed star

## Must include
- Mediterranean lifestyle and a sense of place
- Shared social moments
- Sunlight / outdoor atmosphere when seasonally relevant
- Clear but understated product presence
- Responsible-drinking framing

## Must avoid
- Excessive or irresponsible drinking
- Any underage appeal or targeting
- Unsafe behaviour (e.g. drinking and driving)
- Health or performance claims
- Cold, purely transactional hard-sell tone that breaks the emotional register

## Compliance notes
- Target only adults of legal drinking age in each market.
- Include responsible-drinking cues where appropriate.
- All creative must pass platform alcohol-advertising policy and local regulation review.
